Our Services

Comprehensive Hair
Restoration Treatments

From the latest FUE and DHI techniques to advanced PRP therapy — every treatment is tailored to your unique hair loss pattern, donor density and aesthetic goals.

01
💎

FUE Hair Transplant

The gold standard procedure. Individual follicular units extracted from the donor zone and implanted with sub-millimetre precision — no linear scar, completely natural results.

Learn More →
02
⚡

DHI Direct Implant

Choi pen technology enables simultaneous extraction and placement — no pre-made channels. Higher graft density, faster healing and more natural hairline direction versus standard FUE.

Learn More →
03
🧪

PRP Hair Therapy

Platelet-Rich Plasma therapy uses your own concentrated growth factors to reawaken dormant follicles, strengthen existing hair and significantly accelerate post-transplant graft survival rates.

Learn More →
04
✂

FUT Strip Method

Follicular Unit Transplantation enables the highest graft yield in a single session — the optimal choice for patients with extensive hair loss (Norwood VI–VII) requiring maximum coverage.

🧔

Beard Transplant

Restore density to patchy beards and moustaches with single-follicle FUE grafts placed at the precise angle of natural beard growth — producing a dense, masculine, permanent result.

Frequently Asked

Questions & Answers

The procedure is performed entirely under local anaesthesia — you will feel only mild pressure, never pain. A light oral sedative is available on request. Post-procedure discomfort is mild and resolves within 48–72 hours.
A standard FUE session of 2,000–3,000 grafts takes approximately 6–7 hours. Larger DHI mega sessions may take 8–10 hours. You will be comfortably reclined with meals and entertainment provided throughout the day.
Transplanted hairs shed within the first 2–4 weeks — this is completely normal. New growth begins at months 3–4. Noticeable density appears by months 6–8. Your full permanent result is achieved between 12 and 14 months post-procedure.
Yes — it is the only genuinely permanent solution to hair loss. Grafts are extracted from the DHT-resistant donor zone meaning they are genetically programmed to grow for life regardless of where they are transplanted.
Most patients return to desk-based work within 3–5 days. Visible redness and small crusts resolve within 10–14 days. Strenuous exercise and swimming should be avoided for 3 weeks post-procedure.
Patients with insufficient donor density, certain autoimmune conditions, active scalp infections, or uncontrolled diabetes may not be suitable. Our pre-assessment process identifies candidacy accurately. We will never operate where risk outweighs benefit.
FUE involves extracting grafts and creating recipient channels separately. DHI uses a Choi implanter pen to simultaneously extract and implant — enabling higher density, faster healing and more precise angle control. Dr. Khan recommends the best option based on your individual case.
Yes. We partner with leading Pakistani banks to offer 0% markup instalment plans for eligible patients, typically payable over 6 to 18 months — with full transparency and no hidden charges.
